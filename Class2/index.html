How to Make a Sandwich

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Peanut Butter
  • Jelly

Instructions

  1. Get two pieces of bread.
  2. Using a butter knife, slather peanut butter on one side of piece
  3. Then, slather jelly on one side of the other piece
  4. combine pieces of bread.
Additional Sandwich tips Cut the bread on a diagonol for a fancier look.
